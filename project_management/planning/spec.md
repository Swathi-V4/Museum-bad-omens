# Cabinet of Omens — Full Project Spec

## Goal

Create a one-page digital museum that explores bad luck superstitions across cultures and explains their psychological meaning through a curated, immersive experience.

---

## Concept

The “Cabinet of Omens” presents superstition as both cultural artifact and psychological pattern. Each exhibit combines historical background with interpretation, encouraging users to reflect on how fear and uncertainty shape belief systems.

---

## Scope

### In

* One-page website
* Sections:

  * Hero / Concept
  * Gallery (4 exhibits)
  * Process (curatorial notes)
  * Footer
* Each exhibit includes:

  * Title
  * History explanation
  * Psychological interpretation
* Victorian-inspired visual style (dark, mystical, ornate)
* Responsive layout (desktop + mobile)
* Navigation links that scroll to sections

### Out

* No multiple pages
* No animations or advanced interactivity
* No external data or APIs

---

## Features

* Clean navigation bar with anchor links
* Hero section introducing concept
* Gallery grid of superstition exhibits:

  * Number 13
  * Black Cats
  * Cutting Nails at Night
  * Breaking Mirrors
* Process section explaining design choices
* Styled cards to simulate museum labels

---

## Design Direction

* Victorian fairy painting aesthetic
* Dark jewel tones (purple, deep blue, gold accents)
* Soft glowing gradients
* Elegant serif typography
* Rounded, framed “artifact card” layout
* Museum-like hierarchy and spacing
